Why you should visit the Zoo Garden?

A visit to the Zoo Garden is always a wonderful choice and is also very educational for those who want to spend their free time in the middle of nature, away from the chaos and stress of the city. We invite you, young and old, to discover the beauty that hides in the Zoo, a real Park that can boast a rare variety of animals, birds, reptiles and fish. Over 4200 copies, many of them non-existent in freedom in Romania, and several of them in danger of extinction are waiting for you to know their stories and history. On our site you will find all the information you need to get to the Zoo as well as a lot of news about animals, birds or reptiles both inside the Zoo and around the world through our colleagues from other zoos around the world.
